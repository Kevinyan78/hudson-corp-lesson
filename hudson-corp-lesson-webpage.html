Hudson Corporation Case Study

Lesson Objectives

  • Improve business vocabulary related to marketing and brand management
  • Enhance speaking skills through discussions and role-play
  • Practice listening comprehension
  • Develop critical thinking skills in a business context

1. Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Introduce luxury brands and luggage
  • Name some luxury luggage brands
  • Discuss: What makes a brand 'luxury'?

2. Background Reading (15 minutes)

  • Read background section
  • Comprehension check
  • Key vocabulary: luxury, craftsmanship, market share, imports
  • Create mind map: 'Hudson Corporation'

3. Brainstorming (10 minutes)

  • Pair work: Potential problems for Hudson in Europe
  • Whole class discussion

4. Market Research (15 minutes)

  • Review market research data
  • Practice percentage language
  • Discuss: Reasons for lower market share

5. Listening Activity (15 minutes)

  • Pre-listening: Context introduction
  • First listen: Note key points
  • Second listen: Fill gaps, check understanding
  • Discuss main ideas

6. Role-play (20 minutes)

  • Assign roles: CEO and Marketing Director
  • Choose two marketing strategies
  • Role-play a meeting to agree on strategies
  • Language support provided

Homework

Write a business e-mail (max 150 words) summarizing agreed strategies and suggesting a meeting time.
